How to Stop Bleeding

  • Apply direct pressure to the wound by pressing with a piece of sterile non-adhesive gauze.  If you don't have gauze, use a piece of folded fabric such as a bandana.
  • Maintain steady pressure on the wound, elevating above the heart if possible.
  • If the gauze soaks through with blood, add another layer on top. Never remove the “skin contact layer” gauze, as this will re-expose the wound and disrupt clotting.

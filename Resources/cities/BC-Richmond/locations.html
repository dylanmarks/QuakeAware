Richmond, BC:
Where to Go In An Emergency

 

In the event of an emergency, Tune into CBC 690 AM to find your local Reception Center.

Reception Centers will generally be in one of Richmond's many Community Centers. Make sure you confirm by tuning in to local radio before going to one of these centers, since they may not be safe locations. We are providing this information simply as an informational tool and not as an official directive for meeting in an emergency.
